Kuukibot: tweeting air quality warnings in Texas.

Every hour, the Texas Commission for Environmental Quality publishes air quality monitor readings for sites throughout the state. We analyzed all of the readings from 2015 and determined a warning threshold based on the highest ends of the data distribution.

Benzene: Top 1.5% Threshold 1.4ppb Toluene: Top 1% Threshold 3ppb Ethylbenzene: Top .04% Threshold .6ppb O-Xylene: Top .6% Threshold .5ppb
